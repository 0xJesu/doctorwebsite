Meet Dr. Shruti Babu

Based in the heart of Nagpur, Dr. Shruti Babu is a renowned General Practitioner and a staunch advocate for primary healthcare. She believes that the foundation of a healthy society lies in preventative care and early diagnosis.


With over a decade of experience serving the local community, she combines modern medical practices with the warmth of traditional family doctoring. Her approach is patient-centric, focusing on holistic well-being rather than just treating symptoms.

MISSION & VISION

Advocating for a Healthier Tomorrow

Dr. Shruti Babu is actively involved in health awareness campaigns across Nagpur. She works to educate the public on hygiene, sanitation, and the importance of regular health screenings.

Her initiative "Health First Nagpur" aims to provide free basic check-ups to underprivileged sectors of the city.
